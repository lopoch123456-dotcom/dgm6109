Relationship between Decision Window and psychological satisfaction

The Transaction Amount (represented by circle size) will show that larger purchases require longer decision windows to maintain high satisfaction, while Spending Category (represented by color) will indicate that "Spark" purchases are more impulsive than "Survival" purchases.

Why this is my best scatterplot: I believe this is one of the best scatter plots I've ever created because it successfully integrates the four dimensions of my personal tracking data into a clear and easy-to-read visualization. By placing the amount on the X-axis and satisfaction on the Y-axis, it shows my spending habits and satisfaction levels.
